// Features

Why choose nostorage?

A lightweight, privacy-first cleaner that does exactly what you need — nothing more.

Privacy first

Runs entirely on your machine. No accounts, no cloud, no telemetry. Your data never leaves your Mac.

Lightning fast

Scans your entire system in seconds. Select what to clean and reclaim gigabytes instantly.

Open source

Fully transparent codebase on GitHub. Inspect, modify, and contribute. No hidden agendas.

Terminal native

Built for developers who live in the terminal. Keyboard-driven interface with intuitive controls.

Smart scanning

Targets known junk file locations. Browser caches, build artifacts, package caches, and more.

Safe by default

Preview everything before deleting. Nothing is removed without your explicit confirmation.
